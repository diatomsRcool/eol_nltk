Acute Bee Paralysis virus (ABPV or APV) infects Apis mellifera. Ascosphaera apis infects dead, white, 'chalky' larva of Apis mellifera. Aspergillus flavus infects dead, mummified, black, hard brood of Apis mellifera. Aspergillus dematiaceous anamorph of Aspergillus fumigatus infects dead, mummified, black, hard brood of Apis mellifera. Aspergillus niger infects dead, mummified, black, hard brood of Apis mellifera. Black Queen Cell virus (BQCV) infects dead, black larva (queen) of Apis mellifera. Chronic Paralysis virus infects Apis mellifera. Cloudy Wing virus infects Apis mellifera. Deformed Wing virus infects deformed abdomen of adult of Apis mellifera. Israel Acute Paralysis virus (IAPV) infects Apis mellifera. Kakugo virus infects mushroom body of adult (gaurd bee) of Apis mellifera. Kashmir Bee virus (KBV) infects Apis mellifera. Malpighamoeba mellificae infects gut of adult of Apis mellifera. Morator aetatulas infects dead, black, upright larva (capped) of Apis mellifera. Nosema apis infects gut of adult of Apis mellifera. Paenibacillus larvae ssp. larvae infects gut of larva of Apis mellifera.
